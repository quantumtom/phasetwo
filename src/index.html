About RoxRite

  • TW
  • IN

RoxRite, born Omar Delgado Macias, was born in Guadalajara, Mexico and emigrated to the United States at the age of six. Growing up in Windsor, CA, Delgado first experienced local b-boys at age 12 and took to breaking. His effortlessly clean style led to the nickname "RoxRite," a term given to him by other b-boys who felt he danced the right way.

With influence from b-boys like Ken Swift, Remind, Thunder and Ground Level, RoxRite has built his own style and a remarkable battling career. He has now won top honors in 100 different competitions across the globe, including the 2011 Red Bull BC One World Championship, completing an unprecedented achievement.

RoxRite is known for his technical ability, showcased through his slick footwork and stick freeze combinations. He’s always strategic, pulling from an endless bag of moves and styles to cater his rounds to exactly what will excite judges. Now, 23 years and 100 wins later, he’s cemented his status as a legend in the game.
